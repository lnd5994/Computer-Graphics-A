Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Instructions: Click and hold down the mouse to move the objects
around the screen. Use the buttons to do the following:
*Reset Location: change the objects back to their original location
*Reset Speed: change the objects back to their original speed
*Faster: speed up the objects' movement
*Slower: slow down the objects' movement
*Stop/Start: if the objects are moving, click to stop them; if the objects
are stopped, click to resume movement (the space bar on your keyboard
functions the same way)
*The up arrow on your keyboard pauses the screen and allows you to
increment the angle viewed with each push
*The down arrow on your keyboard pauses the screen and allows you to
decrement the angle viewed with each push
